After publication of this article [1] it was noticed there were errors in the paper regarding the dose and grade of glucosyl hesperidin due to miscommunications.

The composition of glucosyl hesperidin is reported as “500 mg” but this should instead be listed as “470 mg” throughout the article. Additionally, the G-hesperidin should be reported as containing 74% monoglucosyl hesperidin and residual non-glycosylated hesperidin.

The online version of the original article can be found under doi:10.1186/s12937-016-0123-7.
